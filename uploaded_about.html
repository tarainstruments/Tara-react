Our Journey

The journey of Tara International was started by Dr. Bharatkumar M. Davda back in 1980.
The company has been recognized as an MNC and is associated with many globalized manufacturers as their representative, business partner and technical collaborator in India.

Having started with Agriculture, we reached to a majority of the industrial sector by providing them simple solutions for complex analysis, resolving their QC-QA, R&D, Process Control, In-line, At-line and more.
